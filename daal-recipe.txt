Ingredients:
 - 4 cups water
 - 1 cup lentil -- aka tuval daal
 - 2 tomatoes
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 2 teaspoon tumeric powder
 - 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
 - 1 teaspoon vegetable oil
 - 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
 - one pinch asafoetida

Directions:
 1. Wash daal in cold water, draining mutiple times
 
 2. Chop tomat0es into very small pices.

 3. In a pressure cooker, add washed daal, tomatoes, water, tumeric power, salt.
 
 3. Close cooker; turn on high heat; wait until full pressure is reached; then 
 turn down heat to low and let it simmer for 15 minutes; then turn if off.

 4.  After cooker is cooled, and pressure is low, open it; in another small pan,
 heat the oil, add cumin seeds to have them sputter, add chili powder, and asofoetida.

 5. Add oil to daal as garnish.  Serve.
